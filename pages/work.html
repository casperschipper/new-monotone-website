Thijs Geritz Portifolio App: TGGD+

TGGD+ is the portfolio of projects by Thijs Geritz, a Dutch graphic designer who ventures into other areas of design as well, like illustration, motion graphics, animation and music.

This app showcases Thijs' design for web, theatre, apps, games, film, exhibitions and print. Following Thijs philosophy of "making cool and creative objects regardless of what medium you are working in', the application also includes musical fragments of his work as a sound designer.

The content is dynamic an will be frequently updated so make sure to check back once in a while.
