Thijs Geritz Portifolio App: TGGD+

Designed as a digital portfolio for the dutch graphic designer Thijs Geritz, TGGD+ is an iPad app which explores the different facets of his work: illustration, motion graphics, animation and music.

Following Thijs philosophy of "making cool and creative objects regardless of what medium you are working in', the application also includes musical fragments of his work as a sound designer.

Written natively for the iPad, the application has dynamic content using a customized Content Management System written in PHP.
