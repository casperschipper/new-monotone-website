Monotone


We are an interactive digital studio based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Born from the passion for design and technology, we are specialised in producing interactive applications and digital solutions for a wide range of audiences and mediums without technical compromises.

In the past we have worked designing interactive products and experiences for mobile platforms, web and physical spaces. Please feel free to browse our portfolio or to contact us.

How we work

Holistic

We believe in interdisciplinary and global approaches to design. By doing a sharp and precise analysis of the final goals and strategies, we can reflect on the best way to design, engineer and market a product.

Agile prototyping

Early stages of a product development require the freedom to explore possibilities. Fast prototyping and continuous iteration may assure the success of the final product.

Sustainable implementation

In order to extend, update and enrich the life cycle of a product, sustainability is fundamental. We like to implement technology in a way that future iterations can integrate new components, updates and improvements easily.

Open communication

We love sharing our insights with our clients concerning aesthetics, usability and responsiveness. Sometimes the smallest idea can change the way we perceive technology, trends or business opportunities.
