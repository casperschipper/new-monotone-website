Sonic Paintings

Sonic Paintings is an interactive installation that proposes a new, broadened way to perceive art in a multi-sensory setting. In addition to visual and background information either from painting description, books or audio guides, the viewer will be able to encounter sonic information contained in the painting. The exhibition experience gets enriched by means of stimulate a deeper involvement from the user.

Interaction is achieved by using a movement tracking system in headphones that are worn by the exhibition visitors. This allows the control of the sound perspective based on the view's position in relation to the painting, generating a narrative sonic landscape.

Sonic Paintings was first presented as part of the 'Impressionism: Sensation & Inspiration' exhibition held at the Hermitage Museum, Amsterdam.


Maze Tree

Using cutting edge web technologies such as HTML graphics and Web Audio, Maze Tree is an interactive web application commissioned by the Maze Ensemble and the Holland Festival, Amsterdam.

Upload sounds to an ever-expanding bank of sonic spaces. Maze Tree adds them to a network which you can navigate through in endless ways. Play by choosing paths and listening to the soundscapes created by you and other users.
